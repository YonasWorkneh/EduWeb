About Us

Director's Statement

Eduford has two campuses in New York: the Washington Square campus in Manhattan that is home to the majority of NYU’s undergraduate schools and programs, and the MetroTech campus in Brooklyn that houses the NYU Tandon School of Engineering. Academics are among the best reasons to study in New York. You may enroll in courses at NYU’s undergraduate schools that offer a combined total of over 100 majors and programs. The course offerings provide you with an opportunity to both continue work in your major and investigate new areas of study.
